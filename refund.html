Decided Now

Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2025

We want you to love Decided Now. If something isn't right, we'll make it right.

1. Subscription Overview

Decided Now offers a monthly subscription at $1.99 USD/month. Charges are billed automatically each month on the anniversary of your subscription start date.

2. Free Trial

If a free trial is offered at sign-up, no charge is made during the trial period. You can cancel before the trial ends without being charged. If you do not cancel, the subscription begins automatically at the end of the trial.

3. Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future charges. You retain access to the Service until the end of the current paid billing period — we do not cut access early.

To cancel, use your account settings or contact us at decidednow.app@gmail.com.

4. Refunds

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on your first subscription payment. If you are not satisfied within 7 days of your first charge, contact us and we will issue a full refund — no questions asked.

After the 7-day window, charges are generally non-refundable because the subscription provides immediate access to the Service. However, we evaluate refund requests on a case-by-case basis and may issue refunds at our discretion for:

  • Accidental duplicate charges.
  • Extended periods of Service unavailability due to our error.
  • Other exceptional circumstances, at our discretion.

5. How to Request a Refund

Email us at decidednow.app@gmail.com with:

  • The email address associated with your account.
  • The date of the charge and approximate amount.
  • A brief description of the issue.

We will respond within 3 business days. Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days depending on your bank or card issuer.

6. Chargebacks

We encourage you to contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank. We are committed to resolving issues quickly and fairly. Unresolved chargebacks may result in suspension of your account.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy at any time. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

8. Contact
